About Us

Calm Tree is a small independent nonprofit that runs a mental health website which provides trustworthy content to improve mental health and make healthy changes in you life style.


Problems That We Want To Solve

1.Lack of Awareness – mental health is not synonymous with labelled psychiatric illnesses. Feeling lonely, empty, frustrated, overwhelmed and unhappy without a reason are also mental health issues that can be resolved with talk therapy.

2.Unstructured Ecosystem – system does not emphasize enough on the potential benefits of talk therapy. No platform is able connect enough trained counsellors to all the people in need of therapy.

Lack of Access – affordable talk therapy in a safe environment is not available everywhere.


Solution That We Have Designed

1.State of the art platform – Schedule and connect on high quality video sessions with your therapist from the comfort of your home. Get 24*7 chat access, should you feel the need to reach out.

2.Best in class therapists – Get access to a pool of top psychology experts that are vetted by a 3-step rigorous selection process.

3.Flexibility – Therapy is available to you anytime, anywhere and on any device.

4.Fun Activities - Choosing nature and art as our teachers, weekly assessment of your hobbies.

5.Diversity - A group of variety of therapists from all aspects from age, gender and orientation to choose from. Also an environment with diverse group of people interacting with each other.


The Calm Tree team is a passionate group of people dedicated to providing you with balanced, responsible, helpful, and motivating mental health information, resources, and tools.
